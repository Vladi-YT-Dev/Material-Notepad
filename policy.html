Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of stellar importance to us. This statement explains how Material Notepad manages, respects, and secures your information during use of the application, including integrations with Google API services.

At a Glance (TL;DR)

  • No External Personal Database: All your notes are stored directly on your own device (client-side LocalStorage) or synced to your own Google Drive. We own no servers that store your note contents.
  • Secure API Integration: Connections to Google Drive happen directly from your browser to Google APIS. There is no middleman backend collecting your details.
  • You Are in Control: You can disconnect and remove Google permissions at any time with a single click.

1. Information We Collect and Access

Material Notepad operates as a decentralized, offline-first Markdown text editor. It does not load, register, or upload personal folders or note documents onto third-party backend databases.

When you opt-in to link your Google Workspace Drive Account, we request and utilize the following authentication permissions:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file: Allows the application to create and modify only the specific files (.txt or Markdown notes) you explicitly choose to create, open, or save with this app. We cannot read or manipulate any other files on your Google Drive.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile: Grants temporary client-side access to read your profile name and avatar image URL, purely to render a warm, customized profile view in the applet's user interface.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email: Grants client-side access to view your primary email address to show which active session is signed in.

2. How Your Data is Used

Any information retrieved or stored falls strictly under the following purposes:

We NEVER sell, trade, monitor, share, or analyze any notes or profile data with advertisers, third-party trackers, or machine learning algorithms.

  • Local Persistence: Notes are compiled locally inside your browser's LocalStorage to allow quick offline notes retrieval and immediate edits.
  • On-Demand Syncing: Note uploads/downloads only run when you explicitly click sync. All storage transactions use direct, secure TLS encrypted channels between your home browser and Google Cloud servers.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your notes live purely inside your device's browser and your owned Google Drive:

  • Offline Browser Deletion: You can completely scrub all locally persisted notes and credentials on your desktop by clearing your browser cache/cookies or clicking "Reset All Notes" in the app menu.
  • Google Drive Removal: You can permanently delete the notepad documents from Google Drive at any time, or disconnect Google integration by clicking "Disconnect Google Account" in our Settings panel. You can also revoke access permanently through your Google Account Settings portal.
